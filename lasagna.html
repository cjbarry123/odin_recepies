Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Market Basket Pasta Sauce
  • Parmesean and Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Love

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef in olive oil with minced garlic.
  2. Boil noodles with a pinch of salt.
  3. Add beef, noodles, cheese and sauce to a blender, blend on high til soupy.
  4. Serve chilled in a frosted glass.
